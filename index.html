Hi! This is my personal webpage.

I am a graduate student in the Department of Civil, Structural and Earthquake Engineering at the State University of New York at Buffalo.

Currently, I am pursuing a PhD in Structural Engineering under the guidance of Professors Andrew Whittaker and Ravi Ranade. The primary focus of my research is to study the seismic behavior of structural walls subjected to thermal effects.

Additionally, I am working on the development of a strain hardening cementitious composite for applications in high temperature scenarios.
